About VFOR Store

As the premier scientific marketplace, the VFOR store has defined unparalleled choice and convenience for over a century. We keep science moving forward by offering over 2.5 million products and extensive support services to the scientific research, healthcare, and science education markets in the U.S., Canada, and Europe.


Leading the industry with more than 1,000 product-assistance specialists, plus a purpose-built global warehouse and distribution network designed around the safe handling, storage, and transportation of scientific products and production materials, the VFOR store team delivers the tailored logistics solutions, state-of-the-art inventory management strategies, and on-site operational support you need to work more efficiently and innovate more productively.
